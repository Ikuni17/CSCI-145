About Us

The Louder! Music Foundation believes that music participation is an essential element in the fabric of an enduring society. The organization aims to keep music education alive and available in Bozeman's schools and communities. Founded in 1986, this public charity has reached over 10,000 kids and young adults through its donation of instruments to music education programs. This gives more people the opportunity to make music.

Our Vision

We understand the impact music has on the lives of people of all ages, and the tremendous gift it brings to participants and listeners. We understand the power of music on the soul and the vehicle it provides for our sanity, health and creativity.
