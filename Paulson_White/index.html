Mission Statement

The Louder! Music Foundation is a music charity funded by people who want to strengthen the state of music education, people who believe music is an integral part of society and want to make music more accessible to everyone.
The donations we receive allow us to provide instruments to school music classrooms, and after school programs. We select programs that are in-need, ongoing, sustainable and give more people the opportunity to make music. Our overhead expenses are covered by sales revenue, including authentic celebrity music memorabilia. Our program expenses are covered by donations large and small.
